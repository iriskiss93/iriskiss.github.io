About Iris Kiss

Iris Kiss is a visionary contemporary artist known for her vibrant expression, emotional storytelling, and captivating use of color. Her work blends realism with abstract elements to create immersive visual journeys. Through her paintings and digital art, Iris explores themes of identity, beauty, memory, and the inner landscape of the human spirit.
